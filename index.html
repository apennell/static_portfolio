A bit about me

After several years in New Orleans working as a photographer, photo retoucher, and fine art print specialist, I returned to my roots in the Bay Area and am beginning a career as a full stack web developer. There are so many amazing companies here doing great things and I love the flexibility of industries the programmer skill set allows me to be a part of. I believe being a developer is the best way for me to make a positive contribution to the world. My visual design background gives me a unique perspective and ability that takes my performance to the next level.

I completed General Assembly's Web Development Immersive in June 2015, where I trained to be a full stack web developer. I am currently mastering JavaScript, Node.js, Ruby on Rails, and related frameworks and tools and am excited to take off on my new career path!

Still interested in learning more about me? Want to hear some nice things other people have to say? Curious about my early experiments with HTML, CSS, jQuery, and AJAX? Check out this about me page that I made.
